{% trans "The name Savane is the result of a pun between the surname of its founder and the great African Savannah." %} {% trans "It is a family business with an entrepreneurial and community spirit." %}   {% trans "We have specialized in beef production, animal husbandry and agriculture for generations." %}

{% trans "Our laying hens are fed 65% of locally produced cereals, of which we grow about 10% ourselves and the rest comes from other local farmers within the community." %}
